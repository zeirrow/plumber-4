FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire metro area and surrounding suburbs. Our service radius is approximately 50 miles from downtown, but we can make exceptions for larger commercial projects. Contact us with your address and we'll confirm if you're within our service area.

Do you charge for estimates?

Standard service estimates are completely free. For complex projects requiring detailed inspections or diagnostics, we may charge a small assessment fee that gets credited toward your service if you choose to proceed with us.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), checks, and cash. We also offer financing options through third-party providers for qualifying customers on larger projects.

Are your technicians background-checked?

Absolutely. Every PlumbFix employee undergoes thorough background checks, drug testing, and reference verification before hiring. We also conduct regular random drug testing and continuous training to maintain our high standards.
